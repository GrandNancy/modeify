CarFreeAtoZ is the Washington region's first fully multimodal trip planning and comparison tool. Bike to metro to walk on your way to work? Sick of driving your car to work every day? Want to mix it up? CarfreeAtoZ aims to go beyond traditional approaches to trip planning, taking a more holistic view of connectivity and accessibility.

Developed as part of Arlington County Commuter Services' Transit Tech Initiative, CarFreeAtoZ helps individuals, businesses, and residential communities incorporate smart transportation choices into daily life. By providing outreach and information, ACCS helps make it easier for people to transition from a car-centric lifestyle.

CarFreeAtoZ utilizes open transportation data standards and technologies, including:

  • The OpenTripPlanner multimodal routing engine
  • Street network data for greater Washington/Baltimore area from OpenStreetMap
  • GTFS Transit data feeds from the following providers: ART, DASH, DC Circulator, Fairfax Connector, MTA, PRTC, Ride-On, VRE, and WMATA. Additional providers will be added as their data becomes available.

CarFreeAtoZ is currently in beta. We are continuing to add features and make refinements, and we want to hear your comments and suggestion. To provide feedback:

  • Complete this survey about your overall experience using the site
  • Comment on the usefulness of specific trip using the "rate" link within that option's summary
  • Send any general feedback to feedback@carfreeatoz.com
