CarFreeAtoZ is the Washington region's first fully multimodal trip planning and comparison tool.

Developed as part of Arlington County Commuter Services' Transit Tech Initiative, CarFreeAtoZ makes it easy to adapt smart transportation choices to your daily life.

We are utilizing open transportation data standards and technologies, including:

  • The OpenTripPlanner multimodal routing engine
  • OpenStreetMap
  • GTFS Transit data feeds from the following providers: ART, DASH, DC Circulator, Fairfax Connector, MTA, PRTC, Ride-On, VRE, and WMATA. Additional providers will be added as data is available.

CarFreeAtoZ is currently in beta. We are continuing to add features and would love your suggestions. To provide feedback:

  • Complete this survey about your overall experience using the site
  • Comment on the usefulness of specific trip using the "rate" link within that option's summary
  • Send any general feedback to feedback@carfreeatoz.com
