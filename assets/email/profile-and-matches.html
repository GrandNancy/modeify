You'll be asked a couple quick questions about your travel preferences and your home and work locations. You will then be presented with a series of ranked travel options for that trip, including various combinations of transit, walking, bicycling, and driving.

CarfreeAtoZ utilizes open transportation data standards such as OpenStreetMap and GTFS, as well as the OpenTripPlanner multimodal routing engine. The application currently covers walking, biking, and driving in the greater Washington/Baltimore area, as well as the following transit services: ART, DASH, DC Circulator, Fairfax Connector, MTA, PRTC, Ride-On, VRE, and WMATA. Additional transit providers will be added as data becomes available.
